Bozoma Saint John

Chief Marketing Officer of Nexflix

Bozoma "Boz" Saint John is the Chief Marketing Officer at Netflix. She was named among Top 50 Most Influential Female Leaders in Africa within corporate and business sphere by Leading Ladies Africa, which credits her for being one of their top female leaders to look up too!

Saint John is well-known for her philanthropic efforts, which include serving on the boards of Girls Who Code and Vital Voices. She also represents Pencils of Promise as a Global Ambassador to Ghana; this work earned her placement in President Obama's Advisory Council! And let me tell you something else - she graduated from Wesleyan University with honors (and went there too)!
